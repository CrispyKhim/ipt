Who we are

Felis Catus is an interactive web application created by Christopher Khim. The application aims to help users, like yourselves, gain insights into the world of domestic cats. This web application was developed for the HSC IPT Major Project. A multimedia system was to be developed and programmed to be interactive with various media types including texts, images, videos, audio, and animation.

About Us

What our services offer

Welcome to Felis Catus, your ultimate destination for all things feline! Our website is designed to delight cat enthusiasts with a variety of engaging features:

We offer services that are interactive and engaging to the users.

Some features that are offered are:

  • Random Cat Images: Need a dose of cuteness? Our random cat image generator serves up adorable pictures of cats to brighten your day.
  • Cat Sounds: Immerse yourself in the world of cats by listening to the sounds they make. From purrs to meows, explore the diverse vocalizations of our furry friends.
  • Learn About Cats: Dive into our informative articles to discover fascinating facts about cats. Whether you're interested in their history, behavior, or care tips, our content has you covered.
  • Search Feature: Looking for something specific? Use our search feature to quickly find information, images, or sounds related to cats.

At Felis Catus, we celebrate the charm and mystery of cats, offering a delightful experience for cat lovers everywhere. Enjoy exploring and discovering the wonders of our feline friends!
